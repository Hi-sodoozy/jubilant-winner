About

Specialist Websites allows Medical Specialists to carve out an online presence.

We understand what it takes to build a website, and for consultant doctors it can be difficult to find the time to dedicate time to start on a website - once you know where to start.

Once your website is up, it's just a yearly fee to keep it up, plus any changes outside the scope of your plan.

A simpler way to get online

How it used to be

  • Sign up to an account like Wordpress or Wix
  • Spend hours building your own site by dragging and dropping bits and pieces, or fixing up a template
  • Discover that you need to pay for an additional app
  • Figure out a workaround, or just pay the money
  • Populate the website with content (words, images etc)
  • Research how to buy a domain name
  • Buy the domain name
  • Discover that you need to pay for hosting before you can connect the website you built to the domain you just bought
  • Done. For 12 months. Until you need to renew the subscription.

Cheapest WIX plan to stay online: approx. $252 AUD per year* + any add-ons.

*Indicative only; pricing varies by provider, currency, and chosen add-ons.

How it is with Specialist Websites

  • Choose a template
  • Customise the design (Optional. Pick your own colours, fonts, add/remove pages)
  • Create the content, or leave it to us (Supply photos and other unique details)
  • Tell us your preferred website address (Usually DrFirstnameLastname.com.au if available)
  • Done.

To stay online: $99 AUD per year*

The cost price of some website URLs can be higher, which may affect this rate.
